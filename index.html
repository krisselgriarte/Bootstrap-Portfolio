About Me

I was raised and born in Manila, Philippines. I came to the United States when I was fourteen. I do have a big family, I have five more siblings and a cat named Lucy. Since I grew up in The Philippines, I do speak Tagalog fluently.

I enjoy doing yoga, authentic food, coffee, and traveling as much as I can. I try to be a good example for not only my siblings, but also to my friends and the people around me.
